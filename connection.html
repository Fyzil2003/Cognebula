MERGE MANIA

Event Description:

Dive into an engaging team-building journey, a thrilling challenge that will test your team's collaboration and problem-solving skills. In this adventure, teams of two will work through a series of interconnected images to guess the answer, strengthening bonds as they unravel the mysteries together.

Number of Members per Team: 2

Rules:

1. Teams must consist of exactly two members each.

2. Follow the series of clues to breakthrough through the challenges.

3. Puzzles may vary according to the rounds and the questions will be from different genres such as company name, songs etc.

4. Players can skip 2 questions in first round. After using this choice players cannot continue the game further if they cannot guess the answer. This is applicable for second round but the players can skip the questions only once.

5. Each team will be given 2 minutes in 1st round and 1 minute in 2nd round to guess the answer.

6. Adherence to event rules is essential for a fair and enjoyable experience.

7. The first team with highest points will be the champions.

Expected time limit: 2 hrs

Note

E- certificates will be issued for all the participants.
