TUNE TWIST

Event Description:

Join us for a musical mystery and lyrical fun! Step into the spotlight and test your knowledge of melodies and verses at our Tune Twist event. From iconic classics to chart-topping hits, each round presents a medley of songs spanning genres and eras.

Rules:

1. Team Size : Maximum of 3 members.

2. There will be 3 rounds.

3. Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.

4. The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.

Round 1: Voice Vista

Description : With the help of AI , we change over the song's actual voice with some of the famous celebrity voices. The contestants are challenged to identify the voice of the celebrity .

Rules :

1. Ai voice change-over song will be played.

2. Team should find the celebrity's voice.

3. A short task to be completed before answering , which is revealed on spot.

4. With each correct guess a point is rewarded.

5. With wrong answers chances goes to next team.

6. Teams with higher points will be qualified to next round.

Round 2: Transcendia

Description: Participants will be given the translated English lyrics of a Tamil song and will be tasked with identifying the song within 30 seconds.

Rules:

1. Guess the song from lyrics that is translated in english.

2. Each team will be given lyrics of tamil songs that are translated in english.

3. Each team will be given 30 seconds to answer for each question.

4. Team that gets the maximum point moves to final round.

Round 3: Lyric Hunt

Description: Lyrics of a tamil song will be given and the contestants will have to find the first line of the song.

Rules:

1. This is a face-off round.

2. seconds will be given to answer for each question.

3. The team has to answer within the given time.

4. On exceeding the time limit the team looses their chance.

Note:

E - certificates will be provided for every participants
