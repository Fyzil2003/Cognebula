DESIGN FIESTA

Event description:

Welcome to DesignFiesta, where your creativity takes center stage! Craft your best front-end design based on a surprise theme using any design tool you prefer. Let your imagination run wild and win accolades for your skills!.

Round 1:

This is a single round event. The participants will be given a time limit of 2 hours to complete their front- end design. The Theme will be given at the beginning of the event. The teams with attractive UI/UX will be awarded.

Rules:

1. Team size: minimum of 2 members and maximum of 3 members.

2. Participants can use any tool to develop a web design.

3. Participants will be provided with the system and internet connection. Personal laptops are also allowed.

4. The topic will be given on the day of event.

5. The participants are required to have basic knowledge in design.

6. Plagiarism not allowed.

7. The participants are required to have basic knowledge in design.

8. The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.

Note:

E- certificates will be issued for all the participants.
