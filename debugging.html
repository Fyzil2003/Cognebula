FIXIT FEST

Event Description:

Round 1: Decision Dilemma

1. In this round, teams will face a set of 30 multiple-choice questions covering a spectrum of coding concepts and problem-solving scenarios.

2. Each team will have 30 minutes to unravel the challenges and demonstrate their knowledge and quick thinking.

3. Only the top-scoring teams will advance to the next round.

Round 2: BugHunt

1. Brace yourselves for the Debugging Duel, where each team will be presented with 5 progressively challenging coding problems.

2. Participants must identify and fix bugs in the code provided, showcasing their debugging skills and logical reasoning.

3. Teams will have 60 minutes to navigate through the debugging challenges.

Rules:

1. Teams must consist of 1-2 members. Solo participants are welcome.

2. Team members must collaborate and contribute jointly to solve the challenges.

3. Fair play is essential. Any form of malpractice will lead to immediate disqualification.

4. Mobile phones are strictly prohibited during the event to maintain the integrity of the competition.

5. The team with the highest score with the least time taken will emerge as the FixIt Fest champions.

Note

E- certificates will be issued for all the participants.
